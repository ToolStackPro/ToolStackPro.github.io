About this Recorder

This screen recorder lets you capture your entire screen, a window, or a browser tab—including system audio if available—all in your browser without installing software.

Features include:

  • Live Preview: See your screen as it is recorded.
  • Recording Controls: Start and stop recording with ease.
  • Playback & Download: Review the recorded screen capture and save it as a WebM file.
  • Privacy: All recording is processed locally in your browser.
  • Browser-based: Works on modern browsers that support the Screen Capture API.

Ideal for creating tutorials, demos, presentations, or saving anything happening on your screen quickly.
