BMI Calculator

About this Calculator

This BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator helps you quickly determine your body mass index based on your weight and height. BMI is a widely used measure to assess whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height and can indicate if they are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. This calculator provides instant results to help you monitor your health and fitness. It uses the standard BMI formula: weight (kg) / (height (m))².
