About this Recorder

This simple Video Recorder lets you record video and audio directly from your device’s webcam and microphone using only your browser — without any backend or software installation.

Features include:

  • Live Preview: See yourself before and during recording.
  • Recording Controls: Start and stop recording easily.
  • Playback & Download: Review your recorded video and save it as a WebM file.
  • Privacy: No data leaves your device; recordings are processed locally.
  • Browser-based: Works in all modern browsers with webcam support.

This tool is perfect for capturing quick video messages, demonstrations, or interviews on the fly with no hassle.
